Logistics Operations Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project is a logistics analytics dashboard built in Power BI to analyze shipping operations, delivery performance, profitability, shipping costs, and city-level logistics trends.

Data & Tools

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

Key Metrics & Insights

- Generated 558.5M in total shipping revenue with 190M total profit.
- Processed approximately 981K orders serving more than 10K customers.
- Advance Logistics contributed 58.06% of total profit.
- FMCG was the most profitable category, generating 55.43M in profit.
- Tanta ranked as the leading city for both sending and receiving shipments.
- Aswan recorded the highest average shipping cost.
- Monthly order volume peaked at 103K orders.

Outcome

- Identified the most profitable carriers and product categories.
- Improved visibility into shipping cost variations across cities.
- Supported data-driven decisions for carrier selection and operational optimization.
