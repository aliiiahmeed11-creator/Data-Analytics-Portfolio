Healthcare Services Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

This project is an end-to-end healthcare analytics dashboard built with Power BI, Power Query, and DAX to analyze patient visits, financial performance, operations, doctor performance, and diagnosis trends.

 Data & Tools
- Tools: Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel.
- Data: Patient visits, departments, facilities, diagnoses, revenue, waiting times, satisfaction scores, readmissions.

 Key Metrics & Insights
- 35K patient visits across 5 healthcare facilities and 8 medical departments.
- $159.3M total revenue with a 6.43% profit margin.
- Emergency Department is the highest revenue-generating department (~$48M).
- Hypertension and Diabetes are the most common diagnoses (11K+ visits combined).
- Waiting time analysis highlights Emergency and Cardiology as major contributors to long queues.
- Average patient satisfaction score: 3.43/5 with a readmission rate of 46.7%.

 Outcome
- Enables healthcare stakeholders to identify high-demand departments, optimize resource allocation, monitor patient experience, and evaluate financial and operational performance through KPI-driven, interactive dashboards.

E-commerce Sales Performance Dashboard – Excel

This project is a sales analytics dashboard built in Excel using Power Query and Power Pivot to analyze revenue, orders, product performance, customer contribution, and regional trends.

Data & Tools
- Tools: Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot.
- Data: Orders, products, categories, customers, cities, dates.

Key Metrics & Insights
- AED 531.4M in sales generated from 2,606 orders across 34 products.
- Home & Kitchen is the top-performing category, contributing AED 146.5M in revenue.
- Robot Vacuum is the highest-selling product with AED 34.5M in sales.
- Pune, Jaipur, and Bangalore are the strongest-performing cities.
- Sales peak in February and decline in June, indicating seasonal demand patterns.

Outcome
- Helps business stakeholders focus inventory and marketing efforts on top-performing products and categories, strengthen retention programs for high-value customers, and investigate underperforming regions to optimize sales campaigns.

HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

This project is an HR analytics dashboard built with Power BI, Power Query, and DAX to monitor headcount, attrition, performance, employee satisfaction, marital status, and recruitment channels.

Data & Tools
- Tools: Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel.
- Data: Employees, departments, employment status, performance scores, satisfaction levels, recruitment sources.

Key Metrics & Insights
- Workforce of 311 employees across 6 departments.
- 67% active employees and 33% terminated employees.
- Females represent 57% of the workforce.
- Production is the largest department with 209 employees.
- 243 employees fully meet performance expectations.
- Indeed and LinkedIn are the most effective recruitment channels.

Outcome
- Supports HR teams in improving retention strategies, focusing on high-performing recruitment channels, and maintaining employee engagement initiatives to sustain satisfaction and performance levels.
